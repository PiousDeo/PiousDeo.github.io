Casting Machine Duration Improvement

Problem Statement

The casting duration was limited to a few minutes due to thermal instability, feed geometry limitations, and vacuum leakage.

Concepts

Several concepts were explored to improve thermal stability and feed uniformity.

Detailed Design

The redesign included improved thermal interfaces, modified feed geometry, and reinforced vacuum sealing.

Final Assembly

The final assembly resulted in casting duration increasing from minutes to over an hour.
