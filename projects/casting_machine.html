Casting Machine Duration Improvement

This project focuses on increasing casting duration from minutes to over an hour through mechanical redesign, improved thermal interfaces, and vacuum optimization.

Problem Statement

The casting machine suffered from short casting duration due to thermal instability, uneven feed distribution, and vacuum leakage.

Concepts

Several concepts were explored to improve thermal stability and feed uniformity:

  • Redesigned thermal interface plates
  • Modified feed geometry
  • Improved vacuum sealing

Detailed Design

The detailed design phase included CAD modeling, FEA validation, and prototype testing.

Final Assembly

The final assembly integrated all redesigned components, resulting in a casting duration increase from minutes to over an hour.
